Shahat is a town in the Jabal Akhdar region in northeastern Libya. Cyrene was located in the same area in ancient times. It is located 21 kilometers (13 miles) east of Al Bayda. Shahat is connected to Derna by two roads, the first passes through the town of Al-Qubba, which is a mountain road, and the second is a coastal road that passes through Sousse and Ras Al-Hilal.

Sousse is a small Libyan city located on the Mediterranean coast in the Green Mountain, 30 km east and north of the city of Al Bayda. The city is located on the ruins of the ancient city of Apollonia, which is considered an open archaeological museum. The city of Sousse is located in the Jebel Akhdar district.
